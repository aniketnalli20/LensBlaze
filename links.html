About LensBlaze

LensBlaze is a photography-first creative studio run by a photographer and creator. Focused on portraits, events, and brand visuals—with editing and design handled in-house.

  • Real, candid storytelling with thoughtful lighting
  • Clean retouching and consistent color across sets
  • Fast, organized delivery in private galleries
  • Design support for social and branding needs
