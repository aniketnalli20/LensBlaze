About LensBlaze

LensBlaze is built for photographers who want an organized workflow from inquiry to delivery, plus growth features that drive bookings and sales.

  • Deliver client galleries with proofing and controlled downloads
  • Sell prints and digital products with a streamlined checkout
  • Capture leads and manage bookings with structured forms
  • Publish a bio link page that matches your brand

Help Center

Common next steps and quick fixes.

  • For login issues: verify your email, then try again
  • For missing Google accounts: add them in Workspace → Account
  • For sharing: use Workspace → Share link to generate a short URL
